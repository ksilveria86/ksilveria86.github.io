About the Artist

Kati Silveria is a visual printmaking artist and designer based in Spokane, Washington.

In 2021, she received her BA in Visual Communication Design and BA in Studio Art: Printmaking at Eastern Washington University. Some of her work includes an ELLO projects with the Kalispel Tribe, and design work for the Pink Ladies Tournament in Redding, California. In the Spring of 2020, her hand-built relief comic book was exhibited in Eastern Washington University’s Creative Symposium.

Kati has been influenced during her time at EWU by her printmaking professor, Margot Casstevens, and also her design professor, Simeon Mills. They sparked a passion for visual storytelling through comics utilizing the printmaking processes.
